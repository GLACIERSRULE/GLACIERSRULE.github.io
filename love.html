The biopolitics of neo-love: love as factories of biological weapons of the state is pretty obvious, but more sinister is decimation of democratic spaces. Seeing that pink/white/blue box receding from the street, a rowhouse solitary confinement, sneaking off to work or school, making small, too small the solidarity, unity that we are preached. But how could it possibly be enforced? Neo love is a rat race, the overfishing of the many fish in the sea makes sailors and fishermen out of anyone. Neo-love is exploitation of scarcity, neo love is abject commodification, commbined with panic. A common theme in the use of socially internalized oppression is to cause infighting. The obvious logic is that a people who fight amongst themselves cannot organize to figtht back against the monster. This plays out in state sanctioned rooms, where thugs help to inforce whatever damages two oppressed people, once in love, seek to wreck on each other. They are being punished by each other for the break in the states codification of love. Barely anyone ever lifts a finger, most often raised voices are enough to rip open any wounds stitched by love.
