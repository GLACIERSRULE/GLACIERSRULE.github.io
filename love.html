I saw the old sea get caught in its rising, I see the new saw see saw mine in half, and now whenever I look toward horizon, I'm blinded reminding the past.
Declaration on Love
Love has been declared
has been instated
has been codified
is wrapped neatly
a bow covered box (sharp white)
pink and blue checker board
with two or three kids in each box
the to: from: names paid well to be written in stone or diamond
The biopolitics of neo-love: love as factories of biological weapons of the state, but more sinister is decimation of democratic spaces. Seeing that pink/white/blue box receding from the street, a rowhouse solitary confinement, sneaking off to work or school, making small, too small the solidarity, unity that we are preached. "I did it all for my family", held hostage by future sucess, held hostage by unknown duress. Always slipping closer moving motions grappling a top,

But how could it possibly be enforced? Neo love is a rat race, the overfishing of the many fish in the sea makes sailors and fishermen out of anyone. Neo-love is exploitation of scarcity, neo love is abject commodification, commbined with panic. All of this is of course made possibly by a generous grant of constructing time in favor of colonialism and capitalism.
